📖 How to Use

Create a listening exercise in under 2 minutes.

1. Upload a video

Drag and drop or click. Any common format works (MP4, MOV, MKV, WEBM, AVI).

2. Wait for transcript

The transcript appears automatically — no button to click.

3. Edit gaps (optional)

  • Right-click any word → "➕ Add gap" to create a gap (displays as ___).
  • Right-click a gap (___) → "❌ Remove gap" to restore the word.
  • Click "✏️ Edit Text" to correct transcription mistakes before creating gaps.

4. Click "Make it happen"

AI fills in extra gaps based on the level you choose. Your manual selections are kept.

5. Share with students

  • Preview — See exactly what students will see.
  • Student Link — Copy and send. No signup needed.

How students complete the exercise

  1. The video plays the first sentence.
  2. They type the missing words (answers are pre-filled after you create gaps).
  3. They can replay the sentence as many times as needed.
  4. Press Enter or click Next to move to the next sentence.
  5. At the end, they see their score.
