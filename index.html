📖 How to Use the Listening Exercise Builder

Create gap-fill listening exercises from any video and share them with your students with a single link.

🎬 Step 1 — Add Your Video

Choose one of the three options at the top:

  • 🔗 Video Link — Paste a YouTube URL or direct .mp4 link.
  • 📁 Local File — Pick a video from your computer (preview only — students won't see it).
  • ☁️ Cloud Upload — Upload your video to be hosted automatically. Use this if you want to share with students. Supports .mp4, .mov, .avi, .mkv, .webm.
💡 Tip: Cloud Upload takes 1–3 minutes to convert. You'll see a green ✅ when ready.

📝 Step 2 — Generate a Transcript (Optional)

This helps you find the exact times for sections without scrubbing through the video.

  1. Pick a transcript engine (🤖 Auto picks the best for you).
  2. Click ✨ Generate Transcript.
  3. Wait for the transcript to appear with timestamps.
  4. Click any timestamp to jump the video to that point.

✏️ Step 3 — Build Sections

Each section is one play-and-fill task for your students.

  1. Click + Add Section.
  2. Type the sentence(s) in the text box. Use _ (underscore) to mark each gap.
    Example: Today the weather is _ and a bit _.
  3. Type the answers, comma-separated. Example: sunny,cold
  4. Set the start/end times for the audio segment:
    • From the video — pause and click Set Start / Set End
    • From the transcript — click → Start or → End on any line
💡 The number of _ gaps must match the number of answers — a warning shows if they don't.

👀 Step 4 — Preview & Test

  • Preview — See the student view before sharing.
  • ✨ Toggle Gap Highlight — Highlights gaps in your text for visual checking.
  • From preview, click Play Section 1, 2… to test the timing.

🔗 Step 5 — Share with Students

  1. Click Generate Link.
  2. Copy the link that appears.
  3. Send it to students via email, chat, or LMS.
  4. Students open the link and complete the exercise — no signup needed.
⚠️ Important: "Generate Link" only works with YouTube, MP4 links, or Cloud Upload. Local Files cannot be shared.

💾 Saving & Reopening Exercises

  • 💾 Save Exercise — Downloads a .json file you can keep on your computer.
  • 📂 Load Exercise — Reopen a saved .json file later to edit it.

🧑‍🎓 What Students See

  1. The video appears at the top.
  2. They click Play Section 1, Play Section 2, etc.
  3. They type answers in the gaps.
  4. They click Check Answers — green = correct, red = wrong.
  5. After checking, they can click Reveal Answers to see the right ones.
  6. Repeat Section replays the current segment.

🎙️ Transcript Engine Comparison

  • 🐰 Bunny Captions — Best for Cloud Upload videos. Auto-generated, very accurate.
  • 🎯 AssemblyAI — Best for YouTube/MP4 links. ~95% accuracy.
  • 🌐 Browser Speech — Free fallback. Less accurate. Records via microphone while you play the video aloud.

📱 Mobile Support

The app works on phones and tablets. Students can complete exercises from any device with a browser.

❓ Troubleshooting

  • Video won't play after upload — Wait 1–3 minutes for Cloud processing, then refresh.
  • ".mov won't play locally" — Use Cloud Upload instead.
  • Transcript is empty — Try a different engine or wait longer for Bunny captions.
  • Generated link is too long — Keep section text short, or use a link shortener.
